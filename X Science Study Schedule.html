📐 Must-Know Formulas at a Glance

⚡ Electricity

  • V = IR (Ohm's Law)
  • P = VI = I²R = V²/R
  • H = I²Rt (Heating)
  • R_series = R₁+R₂+…
  • 1/R_parallel = 1/R₁+1/R₂
  • 1 kWh = 3.6×10⁶ J

💡 Light

  • 1/v + 1/u = 1/f (Mirror)
  • 1/v − 1/u = 1/f (Lens)
  • m = −v/u (Mirror mag.)
  • P = 1/f (Lens power, D)
  • n = sin i / sin r (Snell's)
  • n = c / v (Ref. index)

💎 Power Tips to Score 90%+
🎨
Use Colour Coding
Use different coloured pens while making notes. Red for definitions, Blue for formulas, Green for examples, Black for general text. Colour improves memory retention by up to 78%.
📐
Perfect Your Diagrams
Every well-labelled diagram can fetch you 2–3 marks. Practice each Biology and Physics diagram 3 times from memory. Examiners give full marks for neat, labelled, titled diagrams.
🧮
Show Every Step
In numerical problems (Physics – Electricity, Light), always write: Given → Find → Formula → Substitution → Answer with unit. You get step marks even if the final answer is wrong.
⭐
Attempt All Questions
Never leave any question blank. Even if unsure, write what you know. Partial answers fetch partial marks. Especially for 3-mark and 5-mark questions – write something relevant always.
💤
Sleep is Non-Negotiable
A well-rested brain recalls information 40% faster. Sleep by 11:00 PM every night. On the night before the exam (24th night), sleep by 10:30 PM. Do NOT pull an all-nighter.
